Jam and Chemicals: Work in Progress Tour 2024

Ever felt like you were on a quest to find home and a sense of belonging? In this new work in progress Dora explores this question.

Creating an entertaining collage of storytelling, original music and playful characters, the show will explore our need for connection and community and ask what happens when it breaks down?

This sharing will be followed by a Q&A. Supported by Arts Council England
